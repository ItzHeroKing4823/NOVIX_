Our Purpose

Our ICT project focuses on raising awareness about the environmental impact of smartphones and how technology affects our planet. Smartphones are used every day, but their production, energy use, and disposal can cause pollution, carbon emissions, and e-waste. This website explores issues such as smartphone manufacturing, electronic waste, and sustainable solutions. Our goal is to educate people about responsible technology use and encourage eco-friendly habits like recycling devices and reducing unnecessary upgrades.

Environmental Impact

The Environmental Cost of Smartphones

E-Waste Problem

Millions of old smartphones are thrown away every year, creating large amounts of electronic waste that pollute the environment.

Carbon Emissions

Producing and transporting smartphones releases carbon dioxide.

Energy Consumption

Smartphones and data centers use large amounts of electricity every day.

Resource Extraction

Mining rare earth materials for smartphone production can damage ecosystems.

Why Should We Care?
